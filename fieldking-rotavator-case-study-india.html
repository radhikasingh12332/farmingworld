Understanding Rotavator in Modern Agriculture

A rotavator (also known as a rotary tiller) is one of the most essential agriculture equipment used for soil preparation.

Why farmers prefer using a rotavator:

  • Saves time compared to traditional ploughing
  • Reduces fuel and labor costs
  • Improves soil aeration and seedbed quality
  • Helps in better crop germination
